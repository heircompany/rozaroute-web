PT Services

Physical Therapy

We'll evaluate your injury, discuss the root cause and come up with your treatment plan on your initial visit. We start with traditional PT to reduce pain, restore function and rebuild mobility to the injured area.

Sports, Personal or Work Injury

Don't let injury keep you from the activities you love. We can help you heal and get back to a normal lifestyle, as well as show you how to prevent re-injury and will provide them with home exercises to continue strengthening and restoring function.

Neuropathy

In most cases neuropathy is predominantly sensory, with pain and sensory loss in the hands and feet. Our Physical Therapists will establish a custom treatment program based on thorough evaluation of your symptoms.

Post Surgery

Post-operative physical therapy is important, and rehabilitation exercises can help increase strength, endurance, motion, balance, and coordination. Our physical therapists focus on the functional goals that are important to you.
